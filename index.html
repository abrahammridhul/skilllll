About Us

About The Event

Skill Bridge 2.0 is a premier technical event designed to equip students with cutting-edge skills in technology and engineering. Through a series of hands-on workshops, expert talks, and interactive sessions, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

About IEEE SB CE Kallooppara

The IEEE Student Branch at College of Engineering Kallooppara is a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts. We are dedicated to fostering technical innovation and professional development among students through various events, hackathons, and seminars.
